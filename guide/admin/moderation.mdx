The moderation section gives you two tools for keeping your seller ecosystem healthy: an approval queue for new product submissions, and a performance dashboard where you can adjust commissions and suspend problematic sellers.

## Routes

| Route | View |
|---|---|
| `/admin/moderacion/aprobacion` | Product approval queue |
| `/admin/moderacion/vendedores` | Vendor performance and controls |

---

## Approval queue (`/admin/moderacion/aprobacion`)

Every product submitted by a seller enters the queue with a `pending` status before it appears in the public catalog.

### Filtering by seller

A dropdown at the top lets you filter the queue by seller name. The default option shows all pending products with a count in parentheses. Each seller option also shows their individual pending count.

### Product cards

Pending products render as a responsive grid of cards. Each card shows:

- Product image (full aspect-square)
- **Pendiente** badge (yellow) in the top-right corner
- Product name, category, and price
- Description (truncated to 2 lines)
- Seller name with avatar initial and submission date
- Three action buttons: **Ver**, **Aprobar**, **Rechazar**

### Reviewing a product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview the product">
    Click **Ver** to open a full-screen modal with the product image, name, price, description, and seller details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve or reject">
    Click **Aprobar** (green) to publish the product to the catalog immediately. The `ProductContext` is refreshed so the catalog updates in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reject with a reason">
    Click **Rechazar** (red) to open the rejection modal. Enter a clear reason (e.g. "Las fotos no muestran claramente el producto") and click **Confirmar Rechazo**. The reason is saved so the seller can address it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Approval syncs the product to the live catalog via `refreshProducts()`. Rejection requires a non-empty reason — the confirm button is disabled until you type one.
</Note>

### Empty state

When the queue is empty a green confirmation card reads **"¡Todo al día!"**.

## Vendor performance (`/admin/moderacion/vendedores`)

This view shows aggregated metrics for every seller and gives you controls to adjust their commission rate or suspend their account.

### Summary cards

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Vendedores** | All users with the `seller` role |
| **Vendedores Activos** | Sellers not currently suspended |
| **Suspendidos** | Sellers with an active suspension |
| **Comisión Promedio** | Mean commission rate across all sellers |

### Sales comparison chart

A recharts `BarChart` compares the top 5 sellers on two axes: **Ventas (miles COP)** (left, purple) and **Pedidos** (right, amber).

### Seller table

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Vendedor** | Avatar initial, name, and email |
| **Ventas** | Total sales in COP |
| **Pedidos** | Order count badge |
| **Comisión %** | Click the value to edit it inline; press Enter or click away to save |
| **Reportes** | Bug report count with warning icon if above 3 |
| **Estado** | Active (green) or Suspendido (red) with reason if suspended |
| **Control** | Toggle button — red to suspend, green to reactivate |

### Suspending a seller

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the suspend toggle">
    In the Control column, click the red toggle for the seller you want to suspend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a suspension reason">
    A modal asks for a mandatory reason (e.g. "Múltiples reportes de calidad de productos"). The confirm button is disabled until the field is filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirmar Suspensión**. The seller's row becomes dimmed and their status badge changes to **Suspendido** with the reason shown below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reactivating a seller

For a suspended seller, click the green toggle in the Control column. No reason is required — the suspension is lifted immediately.

### Editing a commission rate

Click the commission percentage value in the **Comisión %** column. An inline number input appears. Enter the new rate (numeric) and press Enter or click away. The new rate is saved immediately.
