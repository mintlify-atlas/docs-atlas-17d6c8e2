The product management view lives at `/vendedor/productos` (rendered inside the main seller dashboard at `/vendedor`). It shows every fabric listing that belongs to your seller account and lets you update details or remove items.

## Route

`/vendedor/productos` — requires role `seller` or `admin`.

## Product listing

The **Mi Inventario** table displays all of your products. Each row shows:

| Column | Details |
|---|---|
| **Producto** | Thumbnail image, product name, and internal ID |
| **Precio** | Unit price formatted in Colombian pesos |
| **Stock** | Current meters available. Shown in red when below 20 m, green otherwise |
| **Categoría** | Fabric category (e.g. Lino, Algodón, Seda) |
| **Acciones** | Edit and delete buttons |

## Adding a new product

Click **Nuevo Producto** in the table header. Fill in the required fields in the creation form:

- **Nombre** — display name shown to customers
- **Precio** — price per meter in COP
- **Stock** — initial stock quantity in meters
- **Categoría** — fabric type or collection
- **Descripción** — product details visible on the catalog page
- **Imagen** — upload a product photo (accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP)

<Tip>
Uploaded images are converted to Base64 on the client and then sent to the API at `PUT /api/products/:id/image`, which returns a served URL. Your listing will always display the hosted URL, not the raw Base64 string.
</Tip>

## Editing an existing product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the edit icon">
    In the **Acciones** column, click the blue pencil icon for the product you want to change. The row switches to edit mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the fields">
    You can change the **name**, **price** (numeric input), **stock** (numeric input), and **image** (file picker). Category is not editable inline — contact an admin to reclassify a product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or cancel">
    Click the green **save** icon to confirm your changes, or the grey **X** to discard them. A success notification confirms the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After saving, the system refreshes both the `MetricsContext` and the `ProductContext` so that the catalog and your dashboard reflect the new values immediately.

## Deleting a product

Click the red trash icon in the **Acciones** column. A confirmation dialog asks you to verify the deletion. Deleted products are removed from the catalog and will no longer appear in future orders.

<Warning>
Deletion is permanent. If you want to temporarily hide a product, set its stock to 0 instead of deleting it.
</Warning>

## Stock indicators

The stock badge in the table uses color to signal urgency:

- **Green badge** — 20 m or more in stock
- **Red badge** — fewer than 20 m (matches the platform-wide `lowStockThreshold`)

For a dedicated low-stock monitoring view, go to [Stock alerts](/guide/seller/stock-alerts).
