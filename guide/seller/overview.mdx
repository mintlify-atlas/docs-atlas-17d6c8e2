The seller dashboard is your central hub for running your store on D&D Textil. From here you can monitor your sales performance, manage your fabric listings, fulfill customer orders, and respond to stock alerts.

<Note>
Both **seller** and **admin** roles can access seller routes. Admins can visit `/vendedor` and all its sub-routes to inspect or manage any seller-facing view.
</Note>

## Route

`/vendedor` — requires role `seller` or `admin`.

## Dashboard overview

When you open the seller panel you see four metric cards at the top:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Mis Ventas Totales** | Cumulative revenue generated by your store |
| **Mis Pedidos** | Total order count, with a subtitle showing completed orders |
| **Ticket Promedio** | Average order value across all your sales |
| **Reportes Recibidos** | Number of quality bug reports filed against your products |

Below the cards a **line chart** displays your sales for the last 7 days. A **top-5 bestsellers** grid shows your highest-selling products by order volume.

The lower section lists your full inventory table ("Mi Inventario") where you can edit or delete products inline — see [Product management](/guide/seller/products) for details.
